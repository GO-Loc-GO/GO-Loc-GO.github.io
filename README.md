## About Me
Xuanhan Zhu received his B.Eng. degree in Internet of Things Engineering from Beijing University of Technology in 2023. He is currently pursuing his Ph.D. at the State Key Laboratory of Networking and Switching Technology, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, under the supervision of Prof. Jinglin Li, Prof. Quan Yuan, and Prof. Guiyang Luo. 

His current research focuses on 3D computer vision and multi-agent collaborative perception for autonomous driving. He is particularly interested in leveraging 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) to develop unified spatial languages (such as GaussianCollab) and optimizing deep learning models for vehicular networks to achieve extreme communication efficiency.
